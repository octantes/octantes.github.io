viñetas

22/12/2024 // kaste

ideas don't belong to us
they are the fruit of a collective substrate: our time and the references we take
with that in mind, not sharing their outcome is an act of individualism
it's not giving back to the world what it gave us
perfectionism is selfish above all else

with the intention of paying tribute to my ideas, of being "worthy of them", i got lost
the path of art is one of healing
but the cycle doesn't complete until we call the work finished
it doesn't end until we share it so we can start again
transformed by the process

perfectionism is a mirror that inverts our purpose:
leading others along that path
by not sharing, the change stays mental
we carry it inside without it expressing outward

viñetas is one of those projects that had been fermenting on my hard drive since 2020
i abandoned it hoping to improve the mix another time and moved on to the next
i got distracted and forgot it, but you can only connect the dots looking backwards

today i choose to upload it as it was saved, to pick up that path again
time to become a channel for those ideas again and the first step is accepting them as they are
it's up to you to see if there's something there and complete the cycle

more soon, as dano says
always things
